Perudo — Where Bluff Meets Brilliance

In the dusty markets of South America, travelers once gathered around worn wooden tables, cups in hand, dice rattling like distant thunder. From these humble beginnings came Perudo — a game where truth is optional, courage is rewarded, and every glance might be a bluff.

Will you speak the truth? Or weave a lie so convincing that no one dares call it? Learn the rules in minutes — but spend a lifetime perfecting your strategy.

How to Play

Each player has a set of dice hidden under a cup. Players take turns making bids on the total number of a certain face showing among all dice in play. Bluffing is key — but beware: if someone calls "Liar!" and you were bluffing, you lose a die.
