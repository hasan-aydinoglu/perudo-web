How to Play

  1. Each player secretly rolls their dice.
  2. Starting player bids a quantity and a face (e.g., “five 3s”).
  3. Next player must raise the quantity or face — or call Liar!
  4. On a Liar call, all dice are revealed. If the bid was wrong, bidder loses a die; otherwise, the caller does.
  5. Last player with dice wins. Optional wild‑1s and palifico rules supported.

Customize rules in Settings: wilds, exact bids, palifico, and more.
